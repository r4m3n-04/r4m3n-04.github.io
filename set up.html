HOW TO SET UP THE THRIVEPOT?

Step 1: Fill the wicking cloth with soil and insert them into the allotted sections of the inner body.

Step 2: Cover the bottom with wicking cloth then fill the rest of the inner body with soil.

Step 3: Make space for your plant and ready it for potting.

Step 4: Pot your chosen indoor plant into the Product.

Step 5: Pour 12 liters of water into the water reservoir.

Step 6: Check the water level indicator and see if it reaches full, once it reaches full the sound of the buzzer will automatically trigger.
